About

About:

This extension runs Fama-French 5-factor regression analysis on stocks using monthly data from Yahoo Finance and regional factor data from Ken French's Data Library.

Factors:

MKT-RF (Market), SMB (Size), HML (Value), RMW (Profitability), CMA (Investment)

Regions:

Region is auto-detected from stock exchange. Supported: North America, Developed Markets, Developed ex US, Europe, Asia Pacific, Japan, Emerging Markets
